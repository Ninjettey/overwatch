Jennifer White

In The Beginning

I was born September 18, 1981 in Hanford, California. I am currently divorced with four children. My children are my life. I do everything I can for them. Including join the military.

Military Life

I joined the US Navy in 2005, and stationed at Norfolk Naval Station HSC-28. After my tour in the Navy, I decided to go into the US Army in 2009 as a Healthcare Specialist or Combat Medic (68W) stationed in JBER Alaska until 2012.

Future Goals

My future goals are pretty cookie cutter. I want to be able to support my family comfortably, in a safe neighborhood and a nice house, without having to look over my shoulder. Having a stable career will be the next step in the right direction to accomplishing that goal.
